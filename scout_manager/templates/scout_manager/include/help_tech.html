Space Types

Definition:

The category that best describe the selected space.

Format:

Equipment Checkout

Walk-up counter where technology items are checked out to students.

Maker Space

DIY space where people can gather to create, invent, and learn. They often have a variety of items, such as 3D printers, software, electronics, craft and hardware supplies and tools.

Production studio

Work space with specialized equipment or technology that suits a particular purpose, such as an audio recording studio, a video editing studio, or a multi-media studio.
