Space Types

Definition:

The category that best describe the environment of the space.

Format:

Cafe

A small counter-service location that serves espresso and other food items.

Food Court

A counter-service location that offers a large area of tables shared with other food court locations.

Food Truck

A counter-service location that serves food outdoors, usually out of a truck.

Market

A space where customers can buy produce, canned items, and other goods to make their own meals at home. Can have a selection of grab and go items, as well as a deli.

Restaurant

Any location that cannot be adequately described by the above.
