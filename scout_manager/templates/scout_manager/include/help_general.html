Hours Notes

Definition:

If a space has predictable alternate hours, include or define those here.

Format:

Free text, no character limit (but be concise!)

How it is used:

This is displayed as plain text and is not searchable, so do not use 24-hour formatting.

Example:

"First Thursday of each month open 7:00am-8:00pm."

Access Restrictions

Definition:

Scout only includes spaces that are available to 100% of students at at least some point during the day. Sometimes spaces are open to all during the day but restricted to students from a specific department evenings and weekends. Other spaces with semi-restricted access include those where classes or events take priority or a key or a key card is required. These spaces can be put into SpaceScout with this "restricted access" field flagged.

When to check the Access Restrictions checkbox:

Check the checkbox if the space is not or may not be available 100% of the open hours, or when entry requires obtaining a key, swiping a card, or similar.

Format:

For Access Notes: Free text, no character limit (but be concise!)

How it is used:

This is displayed as plain text and is not searchable, so do not use 24-hour formatting.

Examples of Access Notes:

"Scheduled courses and programs have priority."

"Husky Card required for access 10pm-midnight Sun-Th."
